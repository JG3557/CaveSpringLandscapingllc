Full-Service Lawn & Landscape Care

Deck Staining

Mulching & Garden Beds

Our Story

Grown From the Ground Up

A push mower, a borrowed weed eater, and a work ethic that hasn't slowed down since.

Bryce Otey founded Cave Spring Landscaping at age 11, not as a summer hobby, but as a real business built on showing up and doing the job right. What began with a single push mower and a family member's borrowed weed eater grew one yard at a time through word of mouth, reliability, and results clients could see from the street.

More than five years later, Cave Spring Landscaping operates with a full client roster and a complete fleet of commercial-grade equipment. The business earned its reputation the hard way: no shortcuts, no subcontractors, just consistent quality that keeps customers calling back year after year.

Every property still gets the same personal care Bryce brought to that very first lawn. You get him and his helper, showing up on time, doing the work right, and standing behind every job.
